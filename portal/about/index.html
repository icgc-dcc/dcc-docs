About

Overview

This is the Portal documentation section which describes the various features of the ICGC Data Portal hosted at https://dcc.icgc.org.

One of most popular features of the portal is Advanced Search which maybe found at https://dcc.icgc.org/search. This is a great place to begin exploring the ICGC data set.

For offline analysis, it is often convenient to work with archive files. These are made available in the Releases feature and is served at https://dcc.icgc.org/releases
